Our Agency

We help leaders create digital experiences that advance their vision for a brighter future.

Eric Brown
Co-founder and CVO
@ericbrwn
Taylor Jones
Co-founder and CEO
@taylorleejones

Whiteboard is a creative agency dedicated to helping visionaries leverage the internet for good. From the time we were 10 years old, my co-founder Eric & I were captivated by the promise of the internet — first as a tool for understanding the world, and as we got older, as a tool for making the world better.

We are all familiar with stories of how the internet can expose the worst of our human nature, but after working with more than 300 clients across 10 years — we believe more than ever that when the internet is engaged with redemptive purpose it offers unprecedented capacity to facilitate positive cultural change.

Whether for new startups that need to bring their ideas to life or for mature organizations in need of digital transformation, we help leaders navigate and execute across the complex digital landscape.

We are organized into three studios that specialize in Digital Branding, Digital Platforms, and Digital Activation giving us the unique capacity to help organizations lead digital end-to-end.

We drive strategy, design brands, develop websites, build apps, launch campaigns, tell stories, train teams, and more.

In all things, we are focused on helping visionaries create digital experiences that advance their vision for the future.
